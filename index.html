About

Support Her Kenya

We are prolife charity group that advocates against abortion and early teenage pregnancy.

we believe in being a voice for the unborn. Our main aim is to uplift the lives of young women in the slum who are vulnerable to teenage pregnancies, child abuse, domestic violence, lack of education and lack of opportunities for self-determination. The specific focus of the organization is embodied in its mission which is: to “reduce the rate of abortion and suicidal deaths attributed to early pregnancies by providing deep peer teaching to young girls and being a solution to a pregnant teenage girl”. We also act as a link between a Pregnant teenage girl and her parents because it is easier for a pregnant girl to talk to someone they perceive as a neutral party – one who is least likely to judge them, punish them or shun them. We counsel and guide any pregnant minor who approaches us and even go as far as mediating the matter with the minor’s parents. This will include helping the minor to break the news to her parents. Our long term goal is to establish a rescue home for pregnant teenage girls where they will be shielded from the stigma and shame associated with early pregnancy. This centers will assist in managing the risky early pregnancies because it will be staffed by qualified and well trained professionals. The rescue centers will provide a community in which the pregnant teens will meet others who are in a similar situation. This will mitigate any loss of esteem suffered when they feel they have made a terrible mistake and fallen behind their peers.
